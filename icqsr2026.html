A featured session on Monday, July 6, bringing together global academic leaders to share visions for how research and education must evolve in the age of AI.

§ 05 — Competition

Data Challenge — Wind Power Forecasting

“Data to Clean Power.” Predict turbine-level output over 288 future time steps (2 days) across two wind farms using limited future meteorological variables.

Registration Deadline
May 30, 2026
Round 1 Submission
June 1, 2026
Final Submission
June 15, 2026
Workshop & Finalists
July 5, 2026

Eligibility & format

Teams of up to 3 participants. Round-1 prediction submission is required to qualify for the final round. Each finalist team must have at least one member registered for ICQSR 2026 and attending the data competition workshop in person on July 5.

Organizing committee

  • Yanting Li — Shanghai Jiao Tong University
  • Sukjoo Bae — Hanyang University
  • Chen Zhang — Tsinghua University

§ 06 — Early Career

Future Faculty Colloquium

The 1st ICQSR Future Faculty Colloquium (formerly the Doctoral Colloquium) is designed for doctoral candidates graduating by Spring 2028 and postdoctoral fellows.

A panel of experts will lead discussions on job search, interviewing tips, career selection, life in academia, work–life balance, and success in teaching, research, and professional service. The event is complimentary for students registered for ICQSR 2026.

Date
Tuesday, July 7, 2026
Application Deadline
June 22, 2026

Co-chairs

  • Yong Chen — University of Iowa
  • Jiaman Wu — City University of Hong Kong
