§ 01 — Letter

From the Chair

Welcome to the 2026 edition of QSR News. This year’s issue centers on ICQSR 2026 in Hong Kong — our flagship international gathering — along with highlights of QSR member achievements over the past year.

The QSR Section continues to grow, with membership exceeding 450 worldwide and active engagement across academia and industry. This year, we are particularly excited about the breadth of programming at ICQSR 2026, the launch of new awards, and the achievements of our members highlighted in this issue.

Hao Yan
INFORMS QSR Section Chair
